# PDF Design Tokens

## Format

Executive: 16:9 landscape.
Formal report: A4 or Letter.

## Spacing

Safe margin: minimum 0.5 inch.
Card gap: consistent.
Footer height: reserved before content.

## Typography

Title: large.
Section: medium.
Body: minimum 10 pt.
Notes: minimum 9 pt.

## Components

- cover block
- KPI card
- risk chip
- flow node
- screen card
- decision board
- QA evidence table

## Color semantics

Green: pass or positive.
Amber: observation.
Red: blocker.
Navy: titles and structure.
Neutral: background.

Fail if color has no meaning or visual hierarchy is flat.
