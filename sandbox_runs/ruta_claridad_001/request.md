# Sandbox Request — Ruta de Claridad UI Architect

Sandbox: SBX_GITHUB_UI_ARCHITECT_RUTA_CLARIDAD_001
Mode: CANDIDATE_READ_ONLY
Profile: UI Architect

## User request base
Design the image prompt for Ruta de Claridad. MarketPlace Libertad Financiera helps people with debt understand their situation, simulate options and move toward an exit plan.

The screen must reduce anxiety, organize information and increase trust.

The user should feel:
- Now I understand where I am.
- I know the next step.
- I am not being charged or pressured.
- I have support and options.
- I can move step by step.

## Route structure
1. Diagnóstico inicial — Ordenamos tu situación sin juzgarte.
2. Mapa de deuda — Convertimos el desorden en un mapa claro.
3. Opciones posibles — Ves caminos posibles antes de decidir.
4. Plan de avance — Sabes qué hacer primero, qué sigue y qué evitar.
5. Claridad ganada — Ya no estás reaccionando; estás siguiendo un plan.
