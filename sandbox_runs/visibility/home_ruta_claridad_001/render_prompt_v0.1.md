# Render Prompt v0.1 — Tu Ruta de Claridad

Status: SANDBOX_VISIBILITY / NO_VALIDATED / NO_RUNTIME
Use only for controlled visual exploration. Do not label as production, Golden Screen or final design.

## Prompt
Create ONE 16:9 desktop web light-mode mockup for the section `Tu Ruta de Claridad` of the fintech product `Libertad Financiera`.

Show only this section or a focused Home slice where this section is the protagonist. It must look like a real premium fintech product section, warm, calm, adult and trustworthy. Not a wireframe, poster, moodboard, dashboard, checkout or simulator.

### Composition
- Warm neutral background.
- Clear title: `Tu Ruta de Claridad`.
- Short subtitle explaining that the path helps the user understand the next steps before checking an offer.
- Main visual: a progressive path/map with 3 to 4 milestone cards connected by a soft curved path.
- The path must feel guided and premium, not like a flat timeline or a checkout process.
- One primary CTA only: `Ver si tengo una oferta`.
- CTA should be visually dominant, trustworthy green, not neon, not aggressive.
- Add a small low-emphasis note near the CTA: alternatives or offers are referential and subject to validation.

### Milestone content direction
Use compact labels similar to:
1. `Ordena tu punto de partida`
2. `Revisa alternativas referenciales`
3. `Avanza al simulador`
4. `Decide con más claridad`

Each milestone may include a very short supportive phrase. Do not add input fields, amounts, dashboards or user data.

### Visual style
- Background: warm neutral, not clinical white.
- Typography: clear, modern, adult fintech.
- Title: navy/deep financial blue.
- Cards: soft radius, light shadow, breathable spacing.
- Accent: minimal warm gold only for progress/goal detail.
- CTA: trustworthy green protagonist.
- Overall tone: human, calm, guided, financially responsible.

### Negative constraints
Do NOT show:
- full Home page;
- mobile layout;
- dark mode;
- simulator form;
- dashboard metrics;
- account widgets;
- DNI, income, phone, debt amount or sensitive data fields;
- guaranteed offer, approval, savings or amount;
- flat timeline;
- checkout steps;
- urgency, countdown, fear or pressure;
- childish gamification;
- Golden Screen, production or validated labels.

### Quality target
The viewer should immediately understand that this section helps them orient themselves and then continue to `/simulador`, without feeling pressured and without thinking that an offer is guaranteed.
