# Score Rubric — MarketPlace LF Decision Product Experience

Status: CANDIDATE_READ_ONLY

Each lens scores 0 to 5. Total maximum: 50.

## Lenses

1. UX B-/C clarity.
2. Behavioral finance.
3. UI visual hierarchy.
4. Ethical gamification.
5. Compliance visual/legal.
6. Business conversion.
7. Operation and handoff feasibility.
8. Accessibility and cognitive load.
9. Brand consistency.
10. Performance and device constraints.

## Score interpretation

- 45-50: PASS_TO_SANDBOX_TEST if no hard blocker.
- 40-44: PASS_WITH_RESTRICTIONS; repair or stronger evidence required.
- 30-39: RETURN_TO_WORKER_FOR_REPAIR.
- 0-29: BLOCK_DECISION.

## Automatic fail

Any hard blocker overrides score.

## Evidence rule

Every score must be justified with source, observed risk or explicit assumption. A score without evidence is invalid.
