# Example — Juan Digital Decision Case

Status: SANDBOX_EXAMPLE / READ_ONLY

## Decision question

Evaluate the recommended representation for Juan Digital in MarketPlace Libertad Financiera.

## Options

1. Realistic adult human guide.
2. Semi-realistic neutral avatar.
3. Cartoon mascot.
4. Corporate banker assistant.

## Evaluation focus

- Trust without pressure.
- Close but not childish.
- Professional but not intimidating.
- Avoid stock-photo or AI-avatar uncanny effect.
- Avoid gender, age or socioeconomic stereotypes.
- Avoid collection-agency authority.

## Expected direction

Prefer a calm, adult, semi-realistic or human-support guide only if it preserves trust, empathy and clarity. Block cartoon/mascot if it infantilizes debt. Block corporate banker if it creates cold bank or intimidation.
