## Fixed MarketPlace context

MarketPlace Libertad Financiera is a fintech product for people who need to organize debt, review referential simulations and advance with guided support without pressure.

The target includes NSE B-/C users who may experience financial stress, shame, avoidance, low trust, cognitive overload and sensitivity to aggressive debt language.

## Experience principles

- Reduce fear and confusion.
- Create clarity before action.
- Preserve autonomy.
- Avoid pressure, false urgency and collection-agency tone.
- Use human, warm and trustworthy framing.
- Keep the path simple enough for low-friction understanding.
- Treat gamification as ethical guidance, not manipulation.

## Visual principles

- Navy as structural trust.
- Green as protagonist action/progress color.
- Warm neutral light surfaces.
- Minimal blue accent.
- Gold only as sober reward/achievement accent.
- Avoid cold bank, clinic, collection, casino, childish game or generic ad style.
